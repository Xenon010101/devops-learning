A network is a group of two or more devices connected together to share data and resources.

Examples:

Laptop connected to Wi-Fi

Phone accessing the internet

Computer connecting to a server

ðŸ”¹ Types of Networks

LAN (Local Area Network):
Small network like home, office, or college.

WAN (Wide Area Network):
Large network connecting multiple LANs.

Internet:
A global network made by connecting many LANs and WANs together.

ðŸ”¹ Logical vs Physical View

Logical View:
Shows how devices are connected conceptually.

Physical View:
Shows actual physical placement of devices and cables.

In cloud computing, we mostly work with logical views.
