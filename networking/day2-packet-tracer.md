What I Practiced Today
Logical vs Physical View

Logical view shows how devices are connected conceptually.

Physical view shows where devices are placed and how they are physically connected.

This helped me understand why cloud platforms mainly show logical views and hide physical infrastructure.

Devices and Their Roles

PC / Laptop: Acts as a client.

Server: Provides services.

Switch: Connects devices within the same network.

Router: Connects different networks and allows traffic to move outside the local network.

I now clearly understand why a router is required to access the internet.
